Consular Services We Provide

  • Passport Renewal: For Nigerians with expired or soon-to-expire passports.
  • Visa Assistance: Support for Czech nationals and residents traveling to Nigeria.
  • Document Legalization: Legalize Czech or Nigerian documents for official use.
  • Emergency Travel Certificates (ETC): Issued in urgent travel cases.
  • Birth Registration: Register Nigerian children born in Czechia.
  • Attestation and Authentication: Confirm the authenticity of Nigerian documents.
  • Dual Citizenship Registration: Guidance for Nigerians with Czech citizenship.
