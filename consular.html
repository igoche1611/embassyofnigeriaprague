Overview of Consular Services

The Consular Section of the Nigerian Embassy in Prague provides a variety of services to Nigerian citizens and foreign nationals, including passport processing, authentication, and emergency travel documents.

Services Offered

  • Issuance and renewal of Nigerian passports
  • Emergency Travel Certificate (ETC)
  • Authentication and legalization of documents
  • Registration of births, marriages, and deaths
  • Police character certificates
  • Assistance to Nigerian nationals in distress

Passport Services

Applicants are advised to start their passport application online at the official Nigerian Immigration Service website before visiting the embassy for biometric capturing and processing.

Authentication and Legalization

Documents issued in Nigeria must be authenticated by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Abuja before submission to the embassy for legalization. All applicants must book appointments in advance for this service.

Emergency Travel Certificate (ETC)

This service is available to Nigerians who have lost their passports or require emergency travel back to Nigeria. Proof of Nigerian citizenship and police report are required.
