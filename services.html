Consular & Embassy Services

  • Passport Services: Renewal, lost/damaged passport replacement, and new issuance.
  • Visa Assistance: Guidance for Czech and international citizens seeking to visit Nigeria.
  • Birth and Marriage Registration: Official registration of vital events for Nigerian citizens abroad.
  • Emergency Travel Documents: Issued to Nigerians in distress.
  • Authentication & Legalization: Certifying Nigerian documents for use in the Czech Republic.
  • Community Support: Assistance to Nigerian citizens in need or legal difficulty.
