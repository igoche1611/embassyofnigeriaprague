Our Services

We provide a range of consular and diplomatic services to both Nigerian citizens and residents of the Czech Republic.

Passport Services

  • New passport application
  • Passport renewal and reissue
  • Lost/stolen passport replacement

Visa Services

  • Tourist visas
  • Business and diplomatic visas
  • Visa application guidance

Document Authentication

  • Attestation of documents
  • Power of attorney notarization
  • Verification of academic and legal documents

Support for Nigerians

  • Emergency assistance
  • Welfare and legal support
  • Registration of Nigerians abroad
