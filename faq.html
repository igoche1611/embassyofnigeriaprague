Common Questions

What are the embassy's operating hours?

The embassy is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM, excluding Czech and Nigerian public holidays.

How do I apply for a Nigerian visa in Prague?

Visit our Visa page and follow the instructions. You may also contact the consular section via the contact form or email.

Where is the embassy located?

The Embassy of Nigeria is located in Prague, Czech Republic. Please see our Contact page for the full address and directions.

Can I walk in or do I need an appointment?

We recommend scheduling an appointment. For urgent matters, please contact us first to confirm availability.

How can I legalize a document?

Submit your documents through the consular service desk. Legalization typically takes 3–5 business days.
