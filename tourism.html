Explore Nigeria

Nigeria is rich in culture, heritage, and natural beauty. Visit landmarks such as Zuma Rock, Olumo Rock, Erin Ijesha Waterfalls, and experience the vibrancy of Lagos and Abuja.

Traveling to Prague?

Prague is a top destination in Europe with its stunning castles, historic bridges, and cultural festivals. Nigerian visitors can enjoy the Charles Bridge, Prague Castle, and Vltava River cruises.

Visa-Free Travel for Diplomats

Nigerian diplomats may enter Czech Republic visa-free for official assignments. Please contact the consular desk for details.

Cultural Etiquette

  • Greet people respectfully—titles are important in both countries.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious or official sites.
  • Public transport is efficient in Prague—tickets are required before boarding.
