Visa Categories and Requirements

  • Tourist Visa: For short visits, sightseeing, or family visits.
  • Business Visa: For business meetings, conferences, and trade activities.
  • Student Visa: For academic studies in Nigeria.
  • Diplomatic Visa: For official government and diplomatic missions.
  • Transit Visa: For passing through Nigeria en route to another country.

General Requirements:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity.
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Recent passport-size photographs.
  • Proof of travel itinerary or invitation letter.
  • Payment of visa fees.
