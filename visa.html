Visa Information

The Embassy of Nigeria, Prague issues visas to individuals who wish to visit Nigeria for tourism, business, or official purposes.

Types of Visas

  • Tourist Visa: For leisure travel or visiting family and friends.
  • Business Visa: For attending meetings, conferences, or exploring business opportunities.
  • Diplomatic/Official Visa: For diplomats or government officials on official duty.

General Requirements

  • Completed visa application form
  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Flight booking and hotel reservation
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Letter of invitation (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Visit the Nigeria Immigration Portal: immigration.gov.ng
  2. Complete and print the online visa application form
  3. Pay visa fee online
  4. Submit application at the embassy with required documents
  5. Wait for processing and collect your visa
