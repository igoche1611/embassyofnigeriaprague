Visa Information

The Nigerian Embassy in Prague offers various categories of visas for travelers wishing to visit Nigeria. Applicants are advised to read the guidelines thoroughly before applying.

Visa Categories

We process the following visa types:

  • Tourist Visa
  • Business Visa
  • Transit Visa
  • Temporary Work Permit (TWP)
  • Subject to Regularization (STR)

Application Procedure

All applicants are required to apply online via the Nigeria Immigration Service portal. Once completed, bring the printed confirmation, payment receipt, and supporting documents to the embassy.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Visa application form
  • Passport photographs
  • Flight itinerary
  • Invitation letter (for business or visiting visa)

Important Notes
