Visa Information

Types of Visas

  • Tourist Visa: For leisure visits to Nigeria.
  • Business Visa: For business-related visits.
  • Diplomatic Visa: For government officials and diplomats.
  • Transit Visa: For short stays between destinations.

General Requirements

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity).
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of accommodation and return flight.
  • Letter of invitation (if applicable).

How to Apply

  1. Visit the Nigerian Immigration portal: portal.immigration.gov.ng
  2. Fill and submit the visa form.
  3. Pay the visa fee online.
  4. Book an appointment with the embassy.
  5. Attend your appointment with all required documents.
