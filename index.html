Welcome to the Nigerian Embassy in Prague

We are committed to strengthening the bilateral relationship between Nigeria and the Czech Republic, providing consular services, promoting tourism, and supporting Nigerian citizens living in the Czech Republic.

Our Mission

To promote and protect the interests of Nigeria and its citizens in the Czech Republic, enhance diplomatic ties, encourage investment, and support cultural exchange.

Our Services

  • Visa Application and Processing
  • Passport Renewal
  • Document Legalization
  • Assistance for Nigerian Citizens
  • Trade & Investment Promotion
