About the Embassy

The Nigerian Embassy in Prague represents the interests of Nigeria and provides a range of consular services to Nigerians and foreign nationals in the Czech Republic. We are committed to offering efficient and courteous service to all our visitors.

Our Services

  • Visa Services: Information and processing of different visa categories.
  • Passport Renewal: Nigerian passport issuance and renewal support.
  • Document Legalization: Legalization of official Nigerian documents for use in the Czech Republic.
  • Emergency Travel Certificates: For Nigerian citizens in distress or without valid travel documents.
  • Consular Registration: Registration of Nigerians living in the Czech Republic.
